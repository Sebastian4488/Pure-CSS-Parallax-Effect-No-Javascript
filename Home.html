South Sudan is a country located in Northeast Africa, bordered by Sudan, Ethiopia, Kenya, Uganda, Democratic Republic of the Congo, and Central African Republic.
Almost 83 percent of the population lives in rural areas, while 80 percent of the population are income-poor living on less than $1 US a day.
Statistics about South Sudan
- 73% of adults are unable to read or write
- 42% of civilians have no more than primary school education
- 75% of people don't have access to health Services
- 43% of people are unable to provide enough food for their font-family
- 1 in 7 woman die from birth
